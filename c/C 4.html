About Benrock Computer Training Centre

Benrock Computer Training Centre is a premier IT education provider located in Opah-Sarpeiman, Accra. We specialize in delivering quality computer training to equip our students with professional skills for the modern workplace.

Our Mission

To train individuals to become professional secretaries and IT specialists who can establish themselves or excel in various firms through quality education and practical skills development.

Code of Ethics

Attendance Policy

No student shall be absent from class without sufficient reason. Parents/Guardians must clearly state the reason for absence. Unauthorized absence for more than 5 days will lead to removal from the program.

Discipline

Discipline is an integral part of our learning process. We cultivate self-control, respect for traditions, and adherence to institutional laws. Politeness, courtesy in speech and conduct, as well as cleanliness are cherished values at Benrock.

School Uniform

The school uniform is obligatory on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays, as well as official functions. Students must come to school neatly dressed in the prescribed uniform.

Dismissal Policy

Irregular attendance, habitual idleness, disobedience or malpractice in connection with examinations, causes injury to the moral tone of the school and is a sufficient reason for the dismissal of the student.

Parental Cooperation

Parents/Guardians are not allowed to enter the classrooms. They can meet the teachers only after obtaining permission from the principal.
